A Few Words About Me

I am a student and wrestler for the University of Northern Colorado. I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, eating food, staying active/healthy, and watching movies.

Favorite Class

My favorite business-related course so far has been the Selling/Sales class that I am currently taking. The structure of the class is different from any classes I've taken previously, and it's fun to research and imagine being part of an actual marketing team. My favorite non-major-related class was definitely History of Rock and Roll.

Wrestling Backstory

My wrestling career started in 7th grade during middle school. Since then I've found a passion for it, winning state in high school and continuing to wrestle into college. Wrestling has consistently been one of the most difficult things I've ever done, but I think that's why I love it so much.

More About Me

I am going in my last year of school, and I have no idea what I'm doing after college. I have a girlfriend, who is also a senior at Northern Colorado. I also have a 2-year-old Pitbull/Staffy mix.
